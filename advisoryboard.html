Advisory Board


The project is run by Martin Haspelmath (Max Planck Institute for Evolutionary Anthropology) and Stefan Müller (Freie Universität Berlin).

The task of the advisory board is to look at series proposals and give recommendations whether a series should be established or not. It will also get involved when general strategic decisions have to be made.
