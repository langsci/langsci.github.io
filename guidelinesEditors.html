Information for editors

Editors do important work at Language Science Press. They manage the review and publishing process of a book.

User guide

Our user guide explains the tasks of the editors and the interaction with the OMP platform. Refer to the guidelines for details of the reviewing procedure.

Propose series

If you want to establish a new series, please fill out the proposal form. The proposal will be sent to our advisory board. It will take about one month to take a decision.
